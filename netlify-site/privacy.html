1. Introduction

Islam Pro is an Islamic mobile application available on iOS that provides Qibla direction, accurate prayer times, full Quran with audio, Duas, Hadith, Tasbih counter, and other Islamic resources.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Islam Pro mobile application and website (islampro.life). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Key Privacy Principles:

  • ‚úì We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • ‚úì You control your data - you can access, export, or delete it anytime
  • ‚úì We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

Email Address
Required for account creation and authentication. Used to send important notifications and account recovery.
Display Name
Optional. Used to personalize your experience within the app.
Profile Picture
Optional. URL to your avatar image if you choose to add one.
Authentication Provider
Records whether you signed in with Apple, Google, or email/password.

2.2 Location Data

Qibla Direction & Prayer Times: The app requires your device's location to:

  • Calculate accurate Qibla (direction to Mecca)
  • Determine precise prayer times for your location
  • Calculate distance to Mecca

Important: Location data is processed locally on your device and is never stored on our servers. We only store your country and timezone to improve accuracy.

2.3 Device Information

We automatically collect technical information to ensure compatibility and diagnose issues:

  • Device Model: e.g., iPhone 15, iPad Pro (for compatibility testing)
  • Operating System: iOS version (e.g., iOS 18.0)
  • App Version: Version of Islam Pro you're using (e.g., 2.0)
  • Device Language: Your preferred language setting
  • Timezone: For accurate prayer time calculations

2.4 Usage & Analytics Data

We collect aggregated, anonymized usage data to improve the app experience:

App Interactions:

  • App opens, closes, and session duration
  • Screens viewed and navigation patterns
  • Feature usage (Compass, Prayer Times, Quran, Tasbih, etc.)
  • Settings changes (theme, language, notification preferences)
  • Button clicks and user actions

Religious Content Tracking:

  • Prayer Tracking: Which prayers you mark as completed (stored locally and synced to your account)
  • Quran Progress: Last surah/ayah read, reading time, bookmarks with optional notes
  • Tasbih Counters: Dhikr phrases and counts
  • Duas & Hadith: Which content you view or bookmark

Subscription & Purchase Events:

  • Paywall views, purchase attempts, successes, and failures
  • Subscription status (active, expired, canceled)
  • Product IDs and pricing (handled securely through RevenueCat)

2.5 User-Generated Content

  • Quran Bookmarks: Notes you add to bookmarked verses
  • Feedback: Bug reports or feature requests you submit
  • App Preferences: Calculation method, madhab, theme, notification settings, selected Quran reciter/translation

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

Security Measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption (HTTPS)
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data is encrypted in our databases
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization for backend access
  • Regular Security Audits: Periodic review of our security practices
  • Secure Authentication: OAuth 2.0 for Apple/Google Sign In, bcrypt password hashing

Important: No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong, unique password for your account.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

Account Data

Usage Analytics

Prayer & Quran Progress

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

7.1 Access & Portability

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Data Export: Download your data in a portable format (JSON/CSV)
  • How: Email privacy@islampro.life with subject "Data Access Request"

7.2 Correction & Updates

  • Update your profile information in Settings > Account
  • Contact us to correct inaccurate data: support@islampro.life

7.3 Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)

  • Delete Account: Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • What Happens: All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Exceptions: We may retain anonymized analytics and legally required records

7.4 Opt-Out & Preferences

  • Push Notifications: Disable in iOS Settings > Islam Pro > Notifications
  • Email Newsletters: Unsubscribe link in any email or Settings > Notifications
  • Analytics: While we don't offer opt-out (it's essential for app improvement), all data is anonymized

7.5 Object to Processing

You can object to certain data processing (e.g., direct marketing). Contact privacy@islampro.life to exercise this right.

7.6 Restrict Processing

Request temporary restriction of processing in certain circumstances (e.g., while we verify data accuracy).

7.7 Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it anytime. This won't affect processing that occurred before withdrawal.

California Residents (CCPA): You have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. See our CCPA section below for details.

7.8 California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you're a California resident, you have these additional rights:

  • Know: What personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of "sale" of personal information (Note: We do not sell your data)
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

To exercise CCPA rights: Email privacy@islampro.life with subject "CCPA Request"

Response Time: We respond to verified requests within 45 days.
